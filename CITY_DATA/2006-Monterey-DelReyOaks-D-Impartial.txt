IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS OF DEL REY OAKS BY CITY ATTORNEY
BALLOT MEASURE D CONCERNING THE ADOPTION OF A
TRANSACTIONS AND USE TAX

City of Del Rey Oaks Ballot Measure D presents to the electors of the
City of Del Rey Oaks the question whether to adopt and impose, for
a period of not to exceed five years, a one percent (1%) transactions
and use tax, commonly known as sales tax. The five year period would
commence on the first day of the first month in which the tax is collected.
The measure will pass if a majority of those qualified electors casting a
vote on the measure vote "yes.‚Äù

If Measure D passes, the proceeds of the tax will be utilized by the City
of Del Rey Oaks for general governmental purposes.
