IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS BY CITY ATTORNEY
MEASURE G

The following analysis has been prepared by the Ridgecrest City Attorney
in accordance, with Election Code'§§5011. 

Measure G will allow the City to levy and collect a special tax of $40.00 or
less, per dwelling unit per year solely to finance the. acquisition, construction,
maintenance, and operation of City parks. The measure requires approval by
2/3’s of those voting in order to pass. 

At the current number of dwelling units, this tax will generate approximately:$424,000 annually. All proceeds will be deposited in a special fund.
The proceeds will be.expended only to acquire land for City parks, construct
and maintain City park improvements, and operate City parks.
