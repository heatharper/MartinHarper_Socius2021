TOWN OF ROSS
SPECIAL TAX FOR PARAMEDIC SERVICES
IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS BY TOWN ATTORNEY
MEASUREG

If this measure is approved by a two-thirds vote, the special tax for
paramedic services administered by a Joint Powers Authority
authorized by the' voters in 1983, 1991, 1994 and 1998, will be
continued with a maximum annual levy of $30.00 on each living unit
and $30.00 per year per 1,500 square feet of structure on each
developed parcel in non-residential use. The applicable appropriations
limit established pursuant to Article XIIIB of the California Constitution
will be increased in an amount equal to the proceeds of the special tax.
The special tax and the corresponding increase in the appropriations
limit expire on June 30,2007.
