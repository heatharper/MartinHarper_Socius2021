IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS BY CITY ATTORNEY
CITY OF CALIFORNIA CITY
MEASURE A

This measure will authorize the City of California City to impose a special fax on
each parcel of land within the city in the amount of $100/parcel. The proceeds
from this tax levy will be place in a special account and spent only for the following
specified purposes: 30% of the proceeds shall be expended for police services.
25% of the proceeds shall be expended for fire services. 30% of the proceeds
shall be expended for street services. And 15% of the proceeds shall be expended for parks and recreation services. This tax replaces the existing special
tax (which has expired by its terms). This tax can be imposed for five years commencing July 1,2007.
