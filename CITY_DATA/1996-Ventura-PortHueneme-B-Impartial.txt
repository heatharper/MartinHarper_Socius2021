CITY OF PORT HUENEME
CITY ATTORNEY’S IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS

BALLOT MEASURE "B"

The proposed Measure, if passed by the voters, approves into the future the
City’s current utility users tax, at the same tax rate as is now in effect.

A “yes" vote on this measure favors adoption of the utility users tax.

A "no" vote on this measure opposes adoption of the utility users tax.
