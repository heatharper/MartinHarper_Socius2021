IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS BY CITY ATTORNEY
CITY OF CALIFORNIA CITY
MEASURE A

If two-thirds of those voting approve, Measure A will authorize 
the City of California City to levy a yearly special tax. The amount
of the tax will vary from year to year as stated in the measure,
The proceeds of the tax will be deposited into a special fund and expended only for municipal purposes. Approval of this special
tax will also constitute approval of an increase in the appropriations limit for the City by an amount equal to the special tax.
