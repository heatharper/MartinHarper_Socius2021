IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS BY CITY ATTORNEY CITY OF CALIFORNIA CITY  MEASURE N;  If two-thirds of those voting approve, Measure N will authorize the City of California City to levy a one year special tax. The amount of the tax will be $82.96 per lot or parcel for the fiscal year beginning July 1,1995. The proceeds of the tax will be deposited into a special fund and emended only for municipal purposes. Approval of this special tax will also constitute approval of an increase in the appropriations limit for the City by an amount equal to the special tax.
