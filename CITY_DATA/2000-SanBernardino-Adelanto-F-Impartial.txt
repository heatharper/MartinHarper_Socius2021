MEASURE “F”

IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS

If enacted this assessment will allow the City of Adelanto to initiate a Parks and
Recreation Program for the residents of Adelanto. Money raised through this
assessment shall be used for Park and Recreation purposes, and cannot be
used for other City Departments.

The assessment fee will be paid by businesses and residents. Single Family
residences will pay $50.00 per year or $4.17 per month.

Apartment units will pay from $25.00 to $75.00 plus $5.00 per unit.
Industrial/Commercial Businesses will pay $100.00 to $500.00 per year based on
the number of employees.

If enacted the duration of this assessment will be ten years, and the assessment
can not be increased during the ten years.
