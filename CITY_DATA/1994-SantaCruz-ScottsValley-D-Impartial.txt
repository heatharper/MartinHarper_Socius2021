IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS BY CITY ATTORNEY
MEASURE 0

Approval of Measure D ratifies the decision of the Scotts Valley City
Council to execute an agreement with the County of Santa Cruz for
the purpose of sharing transaction and use tax revenues with the
County of Santa Cruz following the approval and implementation of
Measure L to be voted upon in the same election as Measure D.
Measure L authorizes the County of Santa Cruz to levy and collect
a 1/4 cent transaction and use tax for general purposes commencing
in 1997 upon the expiration of an existing 1/2 cent transaction and
use tax.
