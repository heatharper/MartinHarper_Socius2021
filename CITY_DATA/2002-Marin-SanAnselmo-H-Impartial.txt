TOWN OF SAN ANSELMO
SPECIAL TAX FOR PARAMEDIC SERVICES
IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS BY THE TOWN ATTORNEY
MEASURE H

If this measure is approved by a two-thirds vote, the special tax for

Paramedic services administered; by a Joint Powers Authority authorized the voters in 1983,1991, and 1994, will be continued with a maximum annual levy of $30.00 oh each living unit and $30.00 per year per 1,500 square feet of structure on each developed parcel in non-residential used, to be collected in addition to fees for transport to the hospital charged to insurance providers, without collection pursued against uninsured
persons. The applicable appropriations limit pursuant to Article of
the California Constitution will be Increased in an amount equal to the
proceeds of the special tax. The special tax and the corresponding
increase in the appropriations limit expire on June 30,2007.
