IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS BY CITY ATTORNEY
CITY OF CALIFORNIA CITY
MEASURE E

Measure E is a proposition to authorize the City of California City to levy a special
tax in the amount of $120 per parcel per year for ten years. Proceeds of the tax will
be used to cover a portion of the cost of water supply and distribution. Two-thirds
of the voters must vote in favor of the proposition for the proposition to pass.
