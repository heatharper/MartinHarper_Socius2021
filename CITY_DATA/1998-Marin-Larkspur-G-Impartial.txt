

IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS BY CITY ATTORNEY
MEASURE G

If this measure is approved by two-thirds of the voters, the appropriations limit established
for Larkspur, pursuant to Article of the California Constitution, will be continued to be
increased in an amount equal to the proceeds of a special tax for paramedic services
and administered by a joint powers agreement for a period of four years, beginning July
1, 1999 and ending June 30, 2003.
