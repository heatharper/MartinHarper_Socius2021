CITY OF LARKSPUR
SPECIAL TAX FOR PARAMEDIC SERVICES
IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS BY CITY ATTORNEY
MEASURE E

If this measure is approved by two-thirds of the voters, the special
tax for paramedic services provided by the Ross Valley Paramedic
Authority will continue to be imposed for an additional four years at the
same rate of not to exceed $30 per year for each living unit located
within the City of Larkspur, and the appropriations limit will be adjusted
accordingly if necessary. The special tax will become effective on July 1,
2003 and shall terminate on June 30,2007.
