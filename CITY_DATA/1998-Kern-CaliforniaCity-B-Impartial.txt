IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS BY	
CITY OF CALIFORNIA CITY COUNSEL MEASURE B	

Measure B is a special tax measure of the City of California City. If this measure is approved by two-thirds of the voters, the city will be able to levy a special tax for three years commencing July 1,1998. The maximum amount of tax the city can levy is $75 per lot or parcel per year. The city will use proceeds of the special tax to pay for police, fire and recreational services, and to repair streets, parks, sewers and building maintenance.
